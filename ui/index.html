About LinkedFootball

Football is the most-watched sport in the world, which connects billions of fans worldwide. It has been played since the 19th century, which makes it a huge resource of information, from leagues and competitions to players. As the sport keeps evolving, as well as technology, their interaction is being widely explored nowadays, which creates the need for structured football-related knowledge.

This project, named LinkedFootball, aims to mitigate this need, by building a comprehensive knowledge base about football. By taking advantage of the content learned with the “WSDL” course, we seek to organize and present football data in a structured way, by leveraging semantic web technologies and linked data principles. Our goal is to have a knowledge base that establishes meaningful relationships between all the entities of soccer, from teams and players to competitions and titles, providing a rich and complete resource for fans and researchers in a structured and accessible way.

How to Use LinkedFootball

  1. Visit the Competitions page to browse tournaments.
  2. Explore football Teams and their details.
  3. Learn about individual Players and their stats.
  4. Run advanced queries on the SPARQL Query page.

Key Features

  • Linked Data principles for football knowledge.
  • Dynamic SPARQL queries to explore data.
  • Semantic Web technologies for structured insights.
